Here is More to Our System

...Firstly, here's The Problem:

Students who are having their first UJ-GYMNASIUM experience are strugling to keep up with reaching their "dream" body goal because they are not motivated enough. They struggle to use equipments correctly, together with what they need to do in the course of them achieving their body goal.

System Breakers came together and devised a Solution

The system we have created focuses on......


- Providing students with a gym plan created by experts.

- Allows students to follow a plan to get results.

- Get a custom gym schedule to help meet goals.

- A progress bar which allows them to track their progress.

System Stakeholders

Our System consists of two Stakeholders :

  • - MR DARSHAN :

    • - Responsible for checking up on students on how well they are fitting in within the gym

  • - STUDENTS :

    • - Responsible for following their workout schedule and being consistent with it

      - Reponsible for choosing their correct body goal journey

    • In a brief Conclusion,

    We are building a system that provides effective monitoring control for students in accordance of them reaching their targetted body goal. Providing them with the right workout plan for their "dream" body goal selection
