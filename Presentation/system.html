Here is More to Our System

...Firstly, here's The Problem:

Students who are having their first UJ-GYMNASIUM experience are strugling to keep up with reaching their "dream" body goal because they are not motivated enough. They struggle to use equipments correctly, together with what they need to do in the course of them achieving their body goal.

System Breakers came together and devised a Solution

The system we have created focuses on providing students with a list of workouts for each body part, a weekly workout plan, together with a status bar which tracks their progress, providing students with feedback which can keep them motivated to continue enrolling with the gym.

In additon.. monitoring their gym attendance by a way whereby the admin generates a qr-code on a daily basis which will be entered by the students

System Stakeholders

Our System consists of two Stakeholders :

  • - MR DARSHAN:

    • - Responsible for logging gym attendance.

  • - STUDENTS :

    • - Responsible for logging their gym attendance

      -View attendance, view trainers view their gym progress, weekly schedule.

In a brief Conclusion,

We are building a system that provides effective monitoring control for students in accordance of them reaching their targetted body goal, training and mental health. Not only for the organisation to have monitoring capabilities, but to provide a better overview on how well they are reaching their body goal by proving status bar
